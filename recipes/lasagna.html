Description

Indulge in the ultimate comfort food experience with our signature Hearty Homemade Lasagna. A classic Italian favorite meticulously crafted with love and care, this dish is a harmonious blend of flavors and textures that will leave you craving for more.

Layers of tender lasagna sheets embrace a rich and savory Bolognese ragù sauce, made from the finest ground beef, slow-cooked tomatoes, onions, garlic, and fragrant herbs. For our vegetarian friends, we offer an equally delightful option with roasted vegetables and a creamy spinach and ricotta filling. Melted mozzarella and Parmesan cheese lovingly blanket each layer, adding a creamy and delightful finish to every bite. Topped with a golden crust of bubbling cheese, our lasagna is baked to perfection, creating an irresistible aroma that will have your taste buds dancing with delight. Perfectly suited for both intimate dinners and festive gatherings, our Hearty Homemade Lasagna promises to be a memorable centerpiece on your table. Experience the warmth and joy of this timeless Italian classic, thoughtfully prepared to satisfy your cravings and leave you with a satisfied smile. Join us and embark on a culinary journey with our exquisite lasagna — a delicious escape into the realm of comfort and satisfaction.

For the Bolognese Sauce:

  • 500g (1 lb) ground beef (you can also use ground pork or a mix of beef and pork)
  • 1 large onion, finely chopped
  • 4 garlic cloves, minced
  • 800g (28 oz) canned crushed tomatoes
  • 2 tablespoons tomato paste
  • 1 cup beef or vegetable broth
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried thyme
  • 1/4 teaspoon red pepper flakes (optional, for a hint of spice)
  • Salt and black pepper to taste
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil

    • For the Vegetable Option:

  • 2 cups mixed roasted vegetables (such as zucchini, bell peppers, mushrooms, and eggplant)
  • 1 cup baby spinach, chopped
  • 250g (1 cup) ricotta cheese
  • 1 large egg
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • Salt and black pepper to taste

    • Other Ingredients:

  • 12-15 lasagna pasta sheets (oven-ready or precooked)
  • 500g (2 cups) shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • Fresh basil leaves for garnish (optional)
